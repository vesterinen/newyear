Welcome, 2014

Be merry, be bright and snowy.

Long dinners and bonfires. Sauna, wilderness and good friends. Let's welcome 2014 together!

New Year's Eve 31.12.2013, Tammirinteentie 41, Evitskog, Finland.

What

For us New Year's Eve in the countryside with wonderful group of people is already a small tradition and we want to keep the flame burning bright with all of you.

We would love to invite you to spend a magical New Year together with friends over a long dinner, a warm bonfire and a hot Finnish sauna in the Nordic wilderness.

Program for the day

Prepare for Finnish sauna, Werewolfs and townspeople and rolling in the snow. Making snowmen and open fire salmon, great new friends and shared memories.

This year we also have little homework for you. Everyone should prepare a small performance, tell a story or talk about a subject she is oh so very passionate about.

For the midnight itself we have reserved lots of laughter and singing in the silenced wilderness. The way New Years should be.





Where

We will spend the NYE in a lovely summer-house-turned-winter-cottage in the heart of Finnish countryside outside of Helsinki.

Located some 30 minutes from the city center in Evitskog (Everwoods), Kirkkonummi's Vaahtermäki (the Maple Hill) is Linda's summer home. To Ville's dismay it has a working wi-fi, 6 bedrooms, and electricity. Luckily we also have wild deers, woodwork and hopefully loads of snow.
